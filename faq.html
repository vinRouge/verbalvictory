1. How can I become a member?

First, see if it’s really for you. How? By visiting our club. You are most welcome to come to our club even 4 times before you make the decision.

If you decide that Verbal Victory is a place for you, you can talk to VP Membership and fill Membership Application Form. Don’t hesitate to ask if you have any questions about the club.

2. How can I attend the meeting?

You can simply come to visit us and have fun. We meet weekly on Mondays, 7 p.m. in Wyższa Szkoła Bankowa on Ratajczaka 5/7. The meeting is free for newcomers and you can visit Verbal Victory 4 times before you decide whether join the club. During those 4 meetings you get familiar with club members and the manner our meetings are held.

3. I’m afraid of speaking in public, it’s not for me.

Even if you feel insecure when speaking in public, still you can come to Verbal Victory. Here, within supportive people, you can live your first speaking experience. Most of us were scared in the begining. But what is great in Verbal Victory is that now the same people are stage-hungry instead of being stage-fright. Simply give it a try!
