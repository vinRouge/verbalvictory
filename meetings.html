Meet us every Monday 7 p.m

Wyższa Szkoła Bankowa, Poznan
Powstańców Wlkp. 2A
(new BR building)

and join the afterparty to talk and socialize :)

During our meetings you'll be able to:

  • Listen to speeches (usually two of them) prepared according to Toastmasters manuals (basic or advanced)
  • Take part in improvisation (Table Topics) and see how we deal with questions we don't want to answer
  • See how we learn and practice our skills of listening, giving feedback or motivating our teams

A team of evaluators will be there to help you find your strengths and disguise areas you should work on. Evaluators will give you specific examples for improving your speaking and leadership skills.
